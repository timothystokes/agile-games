In this game you have been assigned 3 User Stories to be completed within a two week iteration. Each user story has an estimated complexity (size), estimate business value in $ and a Priority set by the business. Each User Story has between 2 and 3 Tasks with estimated duration in hours. The total hours is equal to the total amount of capacity you have in 10 working days. The objective is to complete all the tasks for all User Stories within the allowed time. For each 1 hour of real time the game will use 1 second in its simulation. 

Constraining how to stay focused on completing work is usually applied using Work in Process (WIP) limits. 

In this game, if only one tasks is in process then you get a boost simulate the benefits of a singular focus. If you have move then one task being worked on concurrently then you get a compounding penalty to simulate the impact of context switching. 

Also watch out for priorities at the User Story level and at Task level as you may be impacted by changing requirements or bugs from late testing.
